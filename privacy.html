Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 07, 2026

At Blintix Studio, our philosophy is "radical simplicity and intense focus". This philosophy extends directly into how we handle your data. We build software that respects your privacy by design, avoiding dark patterns and unnecessary data collection.

1. The Data We (Don't) Collect

Our core principle is simple: we do not collect what we do not strictly need. When you visit our studio website (blintix.store) or use our unauthenticated products, we prioritize anonymity.

  • No Tracking Pixels: We do not use third-party tracking pixels (like Facebook Pixel) to track you across the internet.
  • No Invasive Analytics: We use privacy-preserving, cookieless analytics solely to understand high-level traffic patterns (e.g., total visits), not to track individual user behavior.
  • Mailing List: If you choose to join our Early Access or newsletter, we collect your email address purely for communication purposes. This data is never sold to or shared with data brokers.

2. Our Products

Blintix operates a portfolio of micro-products, including VPNs and developer utilities. While this policy covers the main studio website, each product adheres to the same fundamental privacy baseline. Where specific data must be processed to provide a service (e.g., rendering a ZIP file in ZipIt), that data remains strictly localized or is aggressively flushed from our systems immediately upon task completion.

3. Third-Party Services

We leverage industry-standard infrastructure providers to host our applications and manage our mailing lists. These providers act as data processors and are bound by strict security and confidentiality obligations. They are not permitted to use your data for their own independent purposes.

4. Your Rights (GDPR & Beyond)

Regardless of your geographic location, we respect your digital rights. You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your email address from our systems.
  • Ask questions about how our software handles data natively on your device.

5. Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your data, the quickest way to reach the founder is directly via email.
